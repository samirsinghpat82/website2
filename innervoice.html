AFTER FAILURE

Either you can sit down and do nothing
or stand up conquer your all power,
from your brain and heart
and do everything that you can.
It is not about to prove yourself to anyone else.
It's al about to prove yourself only to you.
Think only one thing,
it's not over,
you are not destroyed.
Its a new beggining.
God give you a body,your mother give
birth to you ,to do not
waste on a bitch or anything else.

Always remeber everything is temporary,
love ,breakup,failure,success
everything is temporary.
This life is very short to think ,
so stop thinking and start doing.
Make your life full of struggles ,
because struggle is the real truth of life.
In every aspect of life you have to struggle.
If you start enjoying these struggles ,then
you can only enjoy your life.
Ofcourse,many times you have to face failure,
take it as a part of success.
Because success comes after failure only have true meaning.
So, let the success and failures to come,accept it and move on. If you stop today,stuck today ,then
you cannot start tomorrow.

So,do labour hard,practice,train yourself,
give time to your self and prove to yourself
who you are.

"Make yourself the real you".



-Samir Singh
